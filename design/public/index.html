Room Temperature

Here’s a fun little thing we’ve been working on. It’s not done, but we wanted you to play with it anyway.

It’s all pretty simple. Use the stars to rate whatever is going on right now. There’s a progress bar to tell you how much time you have left to make up your mind.

Your vote gets added up with those from others in your room, showing you how great a time you’re all having. Or not. ;)

We only have a few rooms right now. When we’re done you may be able to create your own, but this will have to do for now.

Enjoy!
